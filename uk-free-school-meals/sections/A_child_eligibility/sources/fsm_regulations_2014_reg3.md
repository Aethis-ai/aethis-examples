# The Education (Free School Meals) (England) Regulations 2014 — Regulation 3

## Regulation 3 — Persons who are entitled to free school lunches

(1) A person is entitled to a free school lunch if the person is a registered pupil at a relevant school in England and is aged under 16, or is aged 16 to 18 and is a relevant sixth-form pupil.

(2) For the purposes of this regulation—
- "registered pupil" has the same meaning as in section 434 of the Education Act 1996;
- "relevant school" means a school within the categories set out in section 512ZA of the Education Act 1996 (maintained schools, Academies, non-maintained special schools, pupil referral units — not independent schools or home education);
- a child is considered to be "aged under 16" based on their age at the start of the school year.

**Note:** Regulation 3 establishes entitlement for children meeting the age and school-type criteria. The means-tested qualifying criteria (Regulation 4) and universal infant entitlement (Regulation 5) build on this foundation — they do not replace it. A child must meet Regulation 3 before any other route applies.
